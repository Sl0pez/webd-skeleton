Purpose of the Site

The primary purpose of this site is to create a platform where I can display my coding skills and journalistic work so that future employers can find me. The secondary purpose of this site is for me to be able to experiment with different web design concepts and code.

Intended Audience

The intended audience are future employees as well as peers I can work and collaborate with.

Tone of the Site

I want the site to have a casual tone that is not too casual to the point that it is colloquial. It needs to be in the area between uptight/formal and colloquial.

Primary Content

Primary content will be the articles I've written. I'll divide them into different categories such as Sports, Humor, Politics, etc.

Each page including the article pages themselves will have the same navbar from the homepage. The only difference is that there will be an indicator telling the user what category they're currently in. On the top left of each article page there will also be a breadcrumb trail.

Secondary Content

Secondary content will include an About Me page with a short bio and a Contact Me page with my relevant contact information.

The About Me page will be straightforward with a short bio in the center of the page and possibly a picture of myself. The Contact Me page will be similarly minimalist with a link to my email address.
